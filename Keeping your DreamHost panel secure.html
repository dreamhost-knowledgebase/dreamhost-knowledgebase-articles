Increasing panel-access security

In your panel, navigate to the (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Security’) page to perform the following:

  • Change your panel password
  • Enable Multifactor Authentication
  • Change your secret question & answer
  • Enable IP locking
  • Examine and terminate active sessions.

DreamHost recommends that you update your passwords often – every 90 days is a good time frame. If you would like more information about password security, please review the following article:

  • Passwords

Using Account Privileges

Another way to keep your panel secure is to only allow another user specific access only after assigning them account privileges. This allows you to grant access to specific areas in your panel without sharing your login information. You can learn more about this feature in the following article:
