Overview

The following describes how to change the billing plan for various DreamHost services you may be using, such as:

  • Starter Shared and Shared Hosting
  • VPS
  • DreamPress
  • Dedicated Server
  • Paid Email
  • Remixer

Each of the services are described below, as the steps to make changes differ between plan types.

Starter Shared and Shared Hosting

To make changes to your Starter Shared or Shared Hosting plan on the Manage Account page, select an option under the ‘Service Plans’ column. View the following article for further information:

  • Changing your Starter Shared or Shared hosting billing plan

VPS

To modify your VPS plan, navigate to the VPS page. Click the Change Plan button to the right of your current VPS to see your plan options. View the following article for further information:

  • Changing your VPS hosting or billing plan

DreamPress

To make changes to your DreamPress plan, open the Managed WordPress page. Click the Manage button for the plan you want to edit, and then click the Edit/Remove Plan button. View the following article for further information:

  • Changing a DreamPress plan

Dedicated Server

Contact support if you would like to migrate to a different Dedicated server configuration/billing plan. Support will then migrate the data for you. View the following article for further information:

  • How do I add a Dedicated Server

Paid Email

Navigate to the Manage Email page to modify your Paid Email plan. Click the Manage Mail Subscription button to view the available billing plan options. View the following article for further information:

  • Paid email plan

Remixer

If you would like to make changes to your Remixer plan, reach out to the Remixer support team by sending an email to remixer@dreamhost.com or submitting a ticket from the Contact Support page. View the following article for further information.

  • Canceling a Remixer plan
