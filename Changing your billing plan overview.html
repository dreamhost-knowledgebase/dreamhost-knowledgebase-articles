Overview

The following describes how to change the billing plan for various DreamHost services you may be using.

Refunds when changing from a Yearly plan to Monthly plan

Please note that if you change your billing plan from Yearly to Monthly, you are not entitled to a refund. However, an account credit will be provided for the remaining balance. View the following article to view DreamHost's refund policies.

  • Refunds

Viewing your account's current plans

You can find your account's current plans under the Active Plans section on the Manage Account page. View the following article for more information:

  • Manage Account overview

Shared Starter and Shared Hosting

To make changes to your Shared Starter or Shared Hosting plan:

  1. Navigate to the Manage Account page.
  2. Click the Manage button to the right of the current plan.
  3. On the next page, select a billing option.

View the following article for further information:

  • Changing your Shared Starter or Shared Hosting billing plan

VPS

To modify your VPS plan:

  1. Navigate to the VPS page.
  2. Click the Change Plan button to the right of your current VPS to see your plan options.

View the following article for further information:

  • Changing your VPS hosting or billing plan

DreamPress

To make changes to your DreamPress plan:

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the Manage button to open the Domain Settings page, which allows you to adjust various settings for your site.
    • If in Grid view, click the Manage button at the bottom of the website box.
    • If in List view, click the Manage button at the far right of your domain name.
  3. Click the Hosting tab.
    Your DreamPress plan page displays.
  4. Click the Change Plan button.

View the following article for further information:

  • Changing a DreamPress plan

Dedicated Server

Contact support if you would like to migrate to a different Dedicated Server configuration/billing plan. Support will then migrate the data for you. View the following article for further information:

  • Upgrading to a new Dedicated server plan

Paid Email

Use the following instructions if you have a paid email plan:

  1. Navigate to the Manage Email page.
  2. Click the Manage email subscription link to view the available billing plan options.

View the following article for further information:

  • Paid email plan

If you would like to make changes to your Remixer plan, submit a ticket via email.
