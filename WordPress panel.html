The WordPress panel menu has three options:

  • One-Click Installs — Opens the One-Click Installs page in your panel, where you can install and manage WordPress and other apps.
  • Managed WordPress — View signup information and general info about your DreamPress site.
  • DreamPress Resources — View additional resource products to further enhance your DreamPress site.
