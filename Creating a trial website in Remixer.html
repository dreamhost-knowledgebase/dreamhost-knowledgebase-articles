Overview

When you visit the Remixer product page, you can create a free trial website to preview it before creating an account. To start using the interactive widget, navigate to the Remixer product page and scroll down past the main image at the top:

Click a layout in the 'Select a layout to get started' section to test out an interactive widget before you sign up for an account.

The following steps describe how to use the free trial version of Remixer, where you can preview various layouts, colors, images, and fonts on your website.

Step one: Selecting a layout

Select a layout from the three example templates, which you can modify later. You are automatically prompted to choose a color (next step) after you select a layout.

Step two: Choosing a color palette

A green checkmark appears on your color selection. When finished, click 'Choose Images' in the lower right area to proceed to the next step.

You can click multiple times on a particular color choice to toggle through a different set of stock photos, which you can preview within the left 'Site Preview' section.

Step three: Choosing an image style

Select an image style from the various options, which you can modify later.

You can click multiple times on an image style choice to toggle through a different set of stock photos, which you can preview within the left 'Site Preview' section.

When finished, click 'Choose Fonts' in the lower right area to proceed to the next step.

Step four: Choosing fonts

To finalize your website, select a font pairing. When finished, click 'Preview Site' in the lower right area.

Previewing your site

After you make the selections described in the previous steps, click 'Preview Site' to view your website. You are also prompted to create a trial account:

Enter your first name, last name, email address, and password, and then click the green Create account and save site button. A trial account is created and you are auto-redirected to your Remixer Dashboard, where you can continue customizing your new Remixer website (under 'My Sites').

Using your Remixer account

When the Remixer dashboard opens, you are able to edit your website further by clicking 'My Sites' in top right menu area:

If you wish to revisit the project at a later time, you can sign into https://remixer.panel.dreamhost.com with your new Remixer trial account credentials you just created.
