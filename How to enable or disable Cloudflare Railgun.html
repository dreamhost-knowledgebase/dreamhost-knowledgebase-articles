To enable Cloudflare Railgun for your site:

  1. Go to (Panel > ‘Domains’ > ‘Manage Domains’).
    • You must add a hosting plan to your account in order to use any Cloudflare Services. Visit the Adding a domain article for instructions.
  2. Once you add a hosting plan to your domain, click the Edit button to the right of the domain under the ‘Web Hosting’ column.
  3. Scroll down to the ‘Cloudflare Services’ section of the page.
    • Be sure you check the 'Enable Cloudflare on this domain?' check box Cloudflare before proceeding. Visit the Cloudflare article for instructions on how to enable it.
  4. Check the 'Enable Cloudflare Railgun?' check box.
  5. Scroll down and click the Change Settings button to save your changes.

Disabling Railgun

To disable Railgun, repeat the steps above but make sure to uncheck the 'Enable Cloudflare Railgun?' check box.
