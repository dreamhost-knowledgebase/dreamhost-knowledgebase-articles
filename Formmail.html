Overview

Formmail is no longer available for use at DreamHost. This is due to the fact that Formmail is outdated, no longer maintained, and insecure.

What can I use instead?

As a replacement, you can use the alternative form service formspree.io, which is a remotely-hosted free tool that accomplishes the exact same thing and is very easy to configure.
