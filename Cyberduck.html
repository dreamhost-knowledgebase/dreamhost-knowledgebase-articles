Cyberduck is an FTP, SFTP, and WebDav client as well as a cloud storage browser for Mac and Windows. You can use Cyberduck to connect to your DreamObjects account.

The following describes how to use Cyberduck to connect to your DreamObjects account.

How to use Cyberduck with DreamObjects

You can set up your DreamObjects connection inside Cyberduck in two ways:

  • Semi-automatically by downloading a preconfigured connection profile (Recommended).
  • Manually by creating a new profile.

Connecting Cyberduck with DreamObjects semi-automatically using a connection profile

You can find your DreamObjects secret keys in the DreamHost panel. Visit the following article for more information:

DreamObjects Keys
  1. Download the DreamObjects Connection Profile.
  2. Once it’s downloaded, double-click the file to open Cyberduck and then create a new DreamObjects bookmark.
  3. Give your bookmark a nickname and close the window to save it.
  4. Double-click on your newly created bookmark and enter the Access Key and Secret Key for the DreamObjects user/buckets to which you wish to connect.
    • If you are using a Mac, the ‘Add to Keychain’ option appears. If you choose to save your credentials, you won’t be prompted to enter them again.
    Upon successful connection, your buckets appear listed as drives in the interface:

Connecting to Cyberduck manually

  1. Open Cyberduck.
  2. Create a new bookmark by clicking the "+" symbol in the lower left.
    A dropdown menu appears at the top, which defaults to 'FTP (File Transfer Protocol)':
  3. Select ‘Amazon S3’ from the drop-down list.
  4. Give your bookmark a nickname and enter the following:
    • Server: Enter the hostname https://objects-us-west-1.dream.io.
    • Access Key ID: Enter the Access Key for the DreamObjects user/buckets to which you wish to connect, and then close the window to save the connection settings.
  5. Double-click on your newly created bookmark and then enter the Secret Key for the DreamObjects user/buckets to which you wish to connect.
    • If you are using a Mac, the ‘Add to Keychain’ option appears. If you choose to save your credentials, you won’t be prompted to enter them again.
    Upon successful connection, your buckets appear listed as drives in the interface:

Managing DreamObjects data using Cyberduck

Cyberduck makes it very simple to manage your data. Once you’ve connected to DreamObjects, simply click and drag files and folders to and from Cyberduck.
