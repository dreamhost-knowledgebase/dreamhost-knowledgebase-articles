Overview

This article explains how to edit or delete a custom record you have already created by following the instructions in this article:

  • Adding custom DNS records

If you're instead attempting to remove non-custom DreamHost DNS-hosting records, review the following article instead:

  • Setting your domain to 'DNS Only'

Editing a custom DNS record

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the DNS Settings link to open the DNS page.
    • If in Grid view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button under your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
    • If in List view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button to the right of your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
  3. Click the pencil icon to the right of the record. This opens the record for you to edit.
  4. Click Save.

Deleting a custom DNS record

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the DNS Settings link to open the DNS page. page.
    • If in Grid view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button under your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
    • If in List view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button to the right of your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
  3. Click the Delete Record button beneath the record fields.
  4. A message appears asking you to confirm the deletion of this record. Click the Remove button.
  5. A confirmation message confirms its deletion.
