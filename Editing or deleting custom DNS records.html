Overview

This article explains how to edit or delete a custom record you have already created by following the instructions in this article:

  • Adding custom DNS records

If you're instead attempting to remove Non-Editable (non-custom) DNS records, review the following article instead:

  • Setting your domain to 'DNS Only'

Editing a custom DNS record

  1. Navigate back to the Websites page.
  2. Find your domain, and click the three dotsicon.
  3. On the menu that appears, click DNS Settings.
  4. Click the pencil icon to the right of the record. This opens the record for you to edit.
  5. When finished, click Save.

Deleting a custom DNS record

  1. Navigate back to the Websites page.
  2. Find your domain, and click the three dotsicon.
  3. On the menu that appears, click DNS Settings. You'll see the DNS records you previously created.
  4. Click the click the pencil icon to the right of the record.
  5. Click the Delete button beneath the record fields.
  6. A message appears asking you to confirm the deletion of this record. Click the red Remove button.
  7. A confirmation message confirms its deletion.
