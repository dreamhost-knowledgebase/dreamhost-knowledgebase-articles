Overview

The DNS page provides a graphical interface that allows you to make important changes to your domain settings. You can use the DNS page to check your current records, view the propagation status, change your nameservers, and more.

Make sure you understand how DNS works before attempting to make changes to your DNS settings. See the following article for more information about viewing and making changes to your DNS settings at DreamHost:

  • DreamHost DNS overview

Opening the DNS page

To open the DNS page, click the DNS Settings link on the Manage Websites page.

  • If in Grid view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button under your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
  • If in List view, click the 'vertical 3 dots' button to the right of your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.

Domain propagation status

The indicator shows you if the domain is propagated. The following options appear:

  • Propagated (green) — Your site's DNS records have been updated worldwide.
  • Checking (orange) — DreamHost is checking whether your domain's DNS records have propagated worldwide.

DNS Checker

The DNS Propagation Checker allows you to select various records and then instantly check a website's current IP address and DNS record information against multiple nameservers located in different parts of the world. Refresh DNS

Click the Refresh DNS button to refresh your DNS settings. An indicator appears indicating the progress.

Add Record

Click the Add Record button to make changes to your DNS records for a domain. The following options are available:

  • A
  • AAAA
  • CNAME
  • MX
  • TXT
  • SRV (advanced)
  • NS (advanced)

See the following articles for more information:

  • How do I add custom DNS records?
  • Editing or deleting custom DNS records

DNS propagation

Once you have added your custom DNS records, they must then update online. This can take several hours. View the following DNS propagation articles for more information:

  • whatsmydns.net — A popular site that checks DNS records around the world.
  • Viewing your DreamHost DNS records — Use DreamHost's DNS propagation checker in the panel.

Nameservers

Your current Nameservers are listed in this section. Click the Change button to make adjustments to your Nameservers.
