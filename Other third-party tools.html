Overview

There are a variety of third-party content management systems (CMSes), wikis, and other site builders that are compatible with your DreamHost-hosted website.

The following describes various resources that guide you in preparing your site, along with a list of tools available for installation.

Preparing your site

Whether you're updating your DNS records to host an external site, installing an SSL certificate on an eCommerce platform, or setting up a database for a CMS, preparing your site is essential.

The sections below offer links to articles that help you learn more about various DreamHost services you can use to integrate third-party tools.
