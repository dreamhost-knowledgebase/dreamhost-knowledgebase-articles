Disabling AutoPay

  1. Navigate to the Manage Payments page in your panel.
  2. Under the Payment Information section, click the Remove AutoPay button.
    A confirmation message displays with information for you to review.
  3. Click the Remove AutoPay Anyway button.
    The Manage Payments page opens and a success message appears, which confirms that AutoPay has been removed.
