Deactivating the auto-responder

  1. Navigate to the Autoresponders page.
    If an autoresponder is active, a green check appears under the ‘Active’ column:
  2. Click the Edit button to the right.
  3. Uncheck the first checkbox titled ‘Activate Autoresponder’.
  4. Click the Edit Autoresponder button to save changes.
