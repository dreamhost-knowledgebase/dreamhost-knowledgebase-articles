The following describes what to do if you receive an expiring credit card notice from DreamHost.

Why did I receive this?

If you have a credit card on file and it's about to expire, DreamHost sends the following email to you:

This is a courtesy email from DreamHost to remind you to visit the panel and update the expiration date for your credit card on file.

What should I do?

If you wish to continue to use the credit card on file, you must update the expiration date as soon as possible.

To update your credit card:

  1. Navigate to the (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Make Payment’) page in the panel.
  2. Click the 'Modify Credit Card AutoPay" link.
  3. Enter a new expiration date for the credit card on file.
