Overview

This article describes how to view your email headers in the Webmail program.

Viewing headers

  1. Navigate to Webmail at https://webmail.dreamhost.com.
    The login page opens:
  2. Log into your account.
  3. In the main panel, click the email you wish to examine.
  4. Click the More button on the top right.
  5. From the dropdown, choose Show source.
    The email headers appear.
