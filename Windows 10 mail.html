1. Click the 'Start' menu and search for the 'Mail' app.
  2. On the top left click 'Accounts'.
  3. On the top right click '+ Add account'. 
  4. Once opened, click 'Add account'.
  5. Scroll down and click 'Advanced setup'.
  6. Click Internet email.
  7. Enter your information.
    • Email address — Enter your full email address
    • User name — Enter your full email address
    • Password — Enter your email password
    • Account name — Enter a name for this account
    • Send your messages using this name — Enter a name to display when sending messages
    • Incoming email server — This is the name of the DreamHost mail server your email is hosted on. View the 'Email client configuration' article for instructions on how to find this information.
    • Account type — Can be POP or IMAP, but IMAP is recommended.
    • Outgoing email server — This is the name of the DreamHost mail server your email is hosted on. View the 'Email client configuration' article for instructions on how to find this information.
    • Check all the check boxes
  8. Click Sign in.
  9. You should then see it was set up successfully. Click Done.
