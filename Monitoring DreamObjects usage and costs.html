Overview

The Usage Report tab in the panel provides billing and storage details relating to your current DreamObjects service plan.

Viewing usage reports

Navigate to the DreamObjects page in the panel and click the Usage tab.

Several graphs appear that illustrate your storage and billing details.

Scheduling notifications

You can also create rules to schedule notifications when your usage reaches a specific threshold. You can add as many rules as you like, such as Storage or Download usage.

Downloading your usage report

Under the Usage Report section, you can create and download a report based on the following:

  • Monthly Billing
  • DreamObjects Daily Usage

You can then choose a username and time frame to view the report. The User and Timeframe sections are left out if the ‘Monthly Billing’ option is selected.
