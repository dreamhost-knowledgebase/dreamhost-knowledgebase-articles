Overview

This article describes the various billing options for the DreamHost SEO Toolkit and how to change to a different billing option if needed.

Billing options

The DreamHost SEO Toolkit is billed monthly or yearly per website, with an introductory rate for the first year of use. Current prices are available on the following page:

  • SEO Toolkit pricing options in the panel

After you add a plan, the first month or first year's fee (depending on which plan you selected) is added to your invoice immediately.

Introductory rate

The introductory rate is only available once per website. If the SEO Toolkit plan is canceled, and then added to the same website in the future, the introductory pricing will not be available.

How to change your billing option

You can change your billing from monthly to yearly (and vice-versa) at any time.

Changing your billing option for a DreamPress website

  1. Navigate to the Managed WordPress page in your panel.
  2. Click the Manage button to the right of the website you wish to change.
  3. Click the Marketing tab.
  4. In the Search Engine Optimization section, click the Manage Plan button.
    Your current payment plan is highlighted.
  5. Change the payment plan by selecting a new term (monthly or yearly).
  6. Click the Change Plan button to confirm.
    When the Managed WordPress page loads, a 'Success!' notification appears confirming the change.

Changing your billing option for non-DreamPress websites

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the Manage button to open the Manage Websites page, which allows you to adjust various settings for your site.
    • If in Grid view, click the Manage button at the bottom of the website box.
    • If in List view, click the Manage button at the far right of your domain name.
  3. In the Search Engine Optimization section, click the Manage button.
    Your current payment plan is highlighted.
  4. Change the payment plan by selecting a new term (monthly or yearly).
  5. Click the Change Plan button to confirm.
    When the Manage Websites page loads, a 'Success!' notification appears confirming the change.

Changing your billing option while in the introductory period

If your billing option is changed during the introductory period:

  • A prorated credit will be issued for any time remaining on your initial plan (at the introductory rate).
  • A new monthly/yearly charge will be added on the day your change is made (at the regular, non-introductory rate).

Introductory pricing will not carry over to the new billing option you've selected.

If you change the domain name of a WordPress or DreamPress website with the panel tool during the introductory period, the introductory rate is retained.

Changing your billing option after the introductory period

If your billing option is changed after the introductory period has ended:

  • A prorated credit will be issued for any time remaining on your initial plan.
  • A new monthly/yearly charge will be added on the day your change is made.

Cancellations

No refund is available if you decide to cancel your SEO Toolkit plan. However, DreamHost will provide a prorated credit for any remaining time on the canceled SEO Toolkit plan, which you can apply to other DreamHost services.
