Overview

This article explains how to manage your DreamPress website settings in the DreamHost panel. This is where you can manage the website's general settings, files, database, plan, staging website, or change the domain name.

Accessing the DreamPress settings page

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the Manage button to open the Domain Settings page, which allows you to adjust various settings for your site.
    • If in Grid view, click the Manage button at the bottom of the website box.
    • If in List view, click the Manage button at the far right of your domain name.
  3. At the top of the Managed Websites page, click either the Website, Hosting, or Staging tabs to manage your DreamPress site.

Managing your DreamPress settings

The following three tabs located at the top of the Managed Websites page allow you to make adjustments to your DreamPress site and plan.

Website

The Website tab displays a general overview of your website settings. Here you can:

  • Manage website and database files
  • Manage WordPress settings
  • View/Change the PHP version
  • Change your domain name
  • Security features
  • Email
  • SEO

Hosting

The Hosting tab displays your current DreamPress plan.

Click the Change Plan button to view other DreamPress plans and features available for your website.

Staging

The Staging tab allows you to create a staging website. This is a way to safely test changes on your DreamPress site before publishing it live. View the following article for more information about staging:

  • Staging on DreamPress

DNS

The DNS tab lets you view and customize your website's DNS records. View the following article to learn more about adjusting DNS records:

  • Adding DNS
