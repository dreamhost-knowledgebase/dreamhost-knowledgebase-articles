Not all payments are automatically refunded when you cancel DreamHost hosting services. This article discusses when refunds are automatically issued and what payment methods can be refunded.

If I cancel hosting services, will I receive a refund automatically?

DreamHost offers a full refund on shared hosting fees if your plan is canceled within the first 97 days of opening the account. The refund is processed back to the original credit card that was used towards those fees, and is issued back to your card automatically only after you cancel your shared hosting plan within your panel.

DreamHost does not offer refunds for Dedicated server or VPS (private server) hosting fees. The 97-day money back guarantee only applies to shared hosting plans.

Are all payment methods refundable?

Unfortunately, DreamHost does not offer automatic refunds for the following payment methods:

  • PayPal
  • Check (written checks or electronic checks)
  • Money Orders
  • Gift Certificates

If you close your shared hosting plan within 97 days of opening the account and had made a manual payment toward the plan with one of these other methods, please visit the (Panel > ‘Support’ > ‘Contact Support’) page to contact support to further assist you with your refund.

If your account is closed after the 97-day refund period has passed, you do not receive an automatic refund but instead receive a prorated credit on your DreamHost account. This can be used toward any future DreamHost service or domain registration fees.
