Overview

These instructions are intended for an individual whose identity has changed rather than a business or organization that is changing hands. For security reasons, DreamHost recommends creating a new account and migrating the business or organization data to it. View the article linked below for more information:

  • How to move a domain hosting between accounts

The following information provides guidance on how to make changes to personal details within your DreamHost account. Typically, this requires changing your account's contact information on the Profile & Privacy page in your panel. However, in some cases, you may also want to update your payment method on the Manage Payments page and/or your domain registration's WHOIS information on the Manage Registrations page.
