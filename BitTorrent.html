BitTorrent as a protocol allows for peer to peer file sharing of large files. Unfortunately, BitTorrent related processes and software are not allowed on DreamHost Shared servers. This can be seen on the Acceptable Use Policy.

Although there is value in the BitTorrent protocol as a distribution method, it is far too easily (and often accidentally) abused/misused and therefore not allowed on DreamHost Shared hosting services.
