Overview

To log into your DreamHost panel at panel.dreamhost.com, you'll need the following two pieces of information:

  • The original email address you used to create your account
  • The password you created while creating the account

What if I forgot the email address?

If you've forgotten or don't have access to the email address you used to create your DreamHost account, you can contact Technical Support using a form on the dreamhost.com website.

  1. Visit dreamhost.com/support.
  2. Scroll to the How can we help? section.
  3. Click the dropdown and select I can't log in to my DreamHost account.
  4. Fill out the text fields and submit your request.

Please note that if you've forgotten your account's primary email address, you MUST use this form. It's not possible to retrieve the correct email address by contacting Technical Support directly.

What if I forgot the password?

If you remember the correct email address but forgot your panel's password, you can reset it using the following article:

  • Change password (Panel users)
