Overview

If you no longer need a website you've been working on, you can delete it from Remixer.

This article explains how to delete a single site within your Remixer plan. If you instead wish to cancel your Remixer plan, view the following article instead.

  • Canceling a Remixer plan

Deleting a site

  1. On the top right menu, click 'My Sites'.
    A list of sites you've created displays.
  2. Locate the site you wish to delete and click the Settings link under it.
  3. Click the tab titled DELETE THIS WEBSITE.
  4. Click the button titled I understand - please delete my website.
    The site is immediately deleted.
