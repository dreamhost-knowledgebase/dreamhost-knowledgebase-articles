This article describes how to view your email headers in the atmail webmail program.

  1. Navigate to atmail webmail at atmail.dreamhost.com.
    The atmail login page opens:
  2. Log into your account.
  3. In the main panel, click the email you wish to examine.
  4. To the far right, click the arrow icon to open the dropdown menu.
  5. In the dropdown menu, click 'View Headers'.
    The email headers appear:
