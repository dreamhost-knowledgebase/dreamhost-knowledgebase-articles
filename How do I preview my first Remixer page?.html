You can preview your Remixer site before publishing it to a domain. To preview, you need to locate the Cloud ID of your site.

Previewing your site

  1. Log into your Remixer panel.
  2. On the top right, click the 'Websites' icon.
  3. Click the 'Settings' link under your site.
  4. On the settings page, click the 'SEO' tab. Here you'll see your Remixer site URL. It is a long unique address for your site. Copy this and paste into a web browser to preview your site.

Saving your page

Remixer automatically saves your pages. You can view all saved pages by clicking 'Websites' on the top right. The page continuesÂ to exist until you delete it.
