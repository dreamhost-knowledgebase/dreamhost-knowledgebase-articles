GoodSync is an easy, secure, and reliable file synchronizer. The free version is limited to 3 jobs or less and 100 or less files and folders in each job. There is also a paid version with additional features.

Connecting to GoodSync (Windows version)

  1. Launch GoodSync.
    If you're just backing up to DreamObjects, select 'No'.
  2. Click Apply.
    If this is the first instance of GoodSync, The ‘New GoodSync Job’ screen opens:
  3. Enter a job name in the ‘Enter Job Name’ box. You can name it anything you wish. You can also choose between ‘Synchronize’ and ‘Backup’ although the ‘Synchronize’ option is most often used.
  4. Click OK to proceed.
  5. When the main page opens, click the button in the top center titled Please click here to select folder. 
    Click My Computer, then OK. A file explorer window opens:
  6. Select which directory on your local machine you’d like to sync from, and then click OK.
  7. When you return to the main page, click the button in the top center titled Please click here to select folder. This is the option to the right.
  8. In the list, select the ‘Amazon S3’ option. This opens a box to enter your DreamObjects credentials:
    • Address – s3://objects-us-west-1.dream.io/yourbucketname (make sure to enter the name of the bucket you’re connecting to). In the example above, it is 'dreamhostexample'.
    • Access/Secret Keys – You can find these in your panel. View the DreamObjects keys article for further details.

    Make sure the ‘Secure Mode (encrypted)’ box is checked.

  9. Click the Go button on the top right to connect.
  10. Click OK on the bottom right to close this window.
  11. When you return to the main page, click the Analyze button. The folder on your computer is analyzed to locate any files.
  12. When finished analyzing, a list of files will display. Click the Sync button to upload your files to DreamObjects.

The data on your local machine is then synced with your DreamObjects bucket.
