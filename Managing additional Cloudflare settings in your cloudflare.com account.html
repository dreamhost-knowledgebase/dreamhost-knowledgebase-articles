Cloudflare settings

You can access the additional settings within your Cloudflare panel.

  1. Log into your account at https://dash.cloudflare.com.
  2. There are several option on the top menu to choose from. For common speed settings, click the 'Speed' button.
  3. On this page, you can further adjust any Cloudflare controlled speed settings.

For additional information on how to manage the various Cloudflare options, please consult the Cloudflare support page at support.cloudflare.com.
