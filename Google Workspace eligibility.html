Overview

Google changed the name of its G Suite product to Google Workspace. See the following article for more information:

  • G Suite is now Google Workspace

The following describes the eligibility requirements to sign up for Google Workspace at DreamHost.

DreamHost Google Workspace Eligibility

In order to sign up for Google Workspace through your DreamHost account:

  • Your domain must be registered or hosted (either Fully Hosted, Parked, Redirect, or 'DNS Only') within your DreamHost account.
  • You must live in a supported country (see Google's supported country list here).
  • The domain cannot be actively using Google Workspace (or its predecessor, Google Apps).
  • The domain cannot have been used with a Google Workspace trial within the last 90 days.
