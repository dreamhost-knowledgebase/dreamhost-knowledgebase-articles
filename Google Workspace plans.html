Users

Any users you create must be under the same plan for a given domain. This means you cannot have some users on the Business Starter plan and some users on the Business Standard plan for the same domain.

However, you can have different plans for different domains. Domain A could have all users on the Business Starter plan and Domain B could have all users on the Business Standard plan.
