Overview

This article explains how to change the type of VPS plan in your panel. This assumes you have already purchased a VPS plan and wish to change the amount of RAM and Storage to a different amount.

Changing your plan type

  1. Navigate to the VPS page.
  2. Click the Change Plan button to the right of your current VPS.
  3. Click Get This VPS under the plan you wish to change to.
  4. Click the billing plan you'd prefer.
  5. A success message confirms your VPS will be upgraded in just a few moments.

Changing your billing plan

  1. Navigate to the VPS page.
  2. Click the Change Plan button to the right of your current VPS.
  3. Click Change Plan Term button under the plan you wish to change to.
  4. Click the billing plan you'd prefer.
  5. Select your new billing plan.

How am I billed when changing plans?

When you change a VPS plan, the new plan is added to your account, and then a prorated credit is issued for the remaining time on the old plan. This credit will apply to the new charge, and you will be responsible for paying the difference.
