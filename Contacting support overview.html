Phone support

Can I use a phone number to reach DreamHost or technical support?

DreamHost does not have a call-in phone number for technical support. Call backs can be scheduled when contacting support and will be prioritized in our queue.

Are call backs included in the account?

Support is mainly provided via email and LiveChat for free. Call back requests are available but not included in the hosting package, except for some professional services. You'll need to add them separately. Visit the following article for further details:

  • Phone support
