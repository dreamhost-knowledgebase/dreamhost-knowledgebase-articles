Google changed the name of its G Suite product to Google Workspace. See the following article for more information:

  • G Suite is now Google Workspace

This article explains how to delete a user from your Google Workspace plan.

Deleting users

Step 1 — Delete the user in the Google Workspace console

Before deleting a user, make sure you have backed up any necessary data. View the following article for details on what is removed:

  • Delete a user from your organization
  1. Log into your account at https://admin.google.com/example.com.
    The center of the screen lists various menu options such as Users, Apps, Security, Billing, etc.
  2. Click the Users menu option.
  3. Hover over the user you wish to delete. To the right click the More dropdown.
  4. From the dropdown, choose Delete user.
    You then have the option of transferring data to a different user:
  5. Start typing the email address of a different user into the box at the bottom to locate that user.
  6. Click Delete.
    A success message displays, notifying you that your data is transferring to the other user.

Step 2 — Adjust the billing plan (if you are not replacing the user)

  1. Navigate to the Google Workspace page in your panel.
  2. Click the Manage Plan button.
  3. Select a lower amount of users you wish to downgrade to.
  4. Click the Change Plan button. A success message confirms your plan has been updated.
