Overview

You can export the contents of your SSL certificate by manually copying the certificate and key files in your panel to your home computer. Please note that there is currently not an automated way to export your SSL certificate into a single file.

Only Sectigo certificates can be downloaded to use on another server.

Exporting

  1. Navigate to the SSL/TLS Certificates page in your panel.
  2. To the right of your domain, click theÂ Settings button.
  3. On this page you will see your Certificate, RSA Private Key, and Intermediate Certificate.
  4. Click the Copy to Clipboard button next to each, then copy to a local file on your home computer.
