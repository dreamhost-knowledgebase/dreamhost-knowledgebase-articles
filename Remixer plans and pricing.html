Remixer Standard

The Remixer Standard plan includes multiple domain support within Remixer. This means that any domain you wish to host must be hosted on the Remixer platform. There is no access to any other type of hosting plan.

Remixer All Access

The Remixer All Access plan includes all the benefits of Remixer Standard and a Shared hosting plan (includes email accounts and access to a Shared web server). This means it is also included in the Non-Profit discount.

Is Remixer included in my DreamHost plan?

If you have already purchased a Shared hosting plan, Remixer 'All Access' is included at no extra charge.

Remixer is not included in VPS, DreamPress, and Dedicated plans. To use Remixer with your VPS or Dedicated plan, you can purchase a Remixer Standard, Remixer All Access, or a Shared hosting plan. Remixer does not work with DreamPress and is not included with standalone domain registrations.

How to confirm which Remixer plan you've purchased

You can view which Remixer plan you're on in your DreamHost panel. Navigate to the Manage Account page. Here you'll see a Remixer line item. It either says 'Remixer Standard' or 'Remixer All Access'.
