There are two types of Remixer plans to choose from — Remixer Standard and Remixer All Access:

Both of these 'stand-alone' plans assume you have not already purchased a Shared hosting plan.

Remixer Standard

The Remixer Standard plan includes multiple domain support. Annual and multi-year plans include a free one-year domain registration which can be used towards a .com, .info, .life, .net, .online, .org, .store, .tech, or .xyz registration:

Month to month 1 year 3 years
$7.95/month $83.40 ($6.95/month) $178.20 ($4.95/month)

 

Remixer All Access

The Remixer All Access plan includes all the benefits of Remixer Standard and a shared hosting plan (includes email accounts):

Month to month 1 year 3 years
$10.95/month $119.40 ($9.95/month) $286.20 ($7.95/month)

 

Is Remixer included in my DreamHost plan?

If you have already purchased a Shared hosting plan, Remixer 'All Access' is included at no extra charge.

Remixer is not included in VPS, DreamPress, and Dedicated plans. To use Remixer with your VPS or Dedicated plan, you can purchase a Remixer Standard, Remixer All Access, or a Shared hosting plan. Remixer does not work with DreamPress and is not included with standalone domain registrations.

How to confirm which Remixer plan you've purchased

You can view which Remixer plan you're on in your DreamHost panel. Navigate to the (Panel > 'Billing & Account' > 'Manage Account') page. Here you'll see a Remixer line item. It either says 'Remixer Standard' or 'Remixer All Access'.
