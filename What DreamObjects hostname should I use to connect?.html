When connecting to your DreamObjects bucket using a client or script, you should use the following URL:

objects-us-east-1.dream.io

This assumes your bucket is in the EAST coast data center. View the following article to determine where your bucket is currently hosted:

  • What cluster is my DreamObjects data currently on?

If your bucket is still on the old WEST cluster, make sure you migrate your data to the new EAST cluster by Oct 1st 2018.

  • Cluster migration procedure

Old hostname

Older DreamObjects buckets may still be on the old WEST coast cluster using the old hostname of objects-us-west-1.dream.io. This will be deactivated on Oct 1st 2018, so it's highly recommended you migrate your data to the EAST cluster before this date.
