Upload video into Remixer

Any video you wish to add to Remixer must first be hosted on YouTube or Vimeo. You can then use the built-in 'Web Section' to add it to your Remixer page. It's currently not possible to upload a video directly from your computer.

To upload:

  1. Click the 'Media' button on the top right.
  2. Click the 'Video' link to view your options to upload.
  3. Click on any option and upload your images.

Editing video

  1. Edit your Remixer page.

    You can also add video by adding a 'Hero Video' 'Web Section'.

  2. Make sure you've added a 'Web Section' to the page that supports video.

  4. A video toolbar appears.
