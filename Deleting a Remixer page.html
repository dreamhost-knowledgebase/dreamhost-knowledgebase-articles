If you no longer need a page you've been working on, you can delete it from Remixer.

Deleting a page

  1. On the top right menu, click 'Websites'. A list of pages you've created displays.
  2. Locate the page you wish to delete and click the 'Settings' link under it.
  3. On the next page you see three tabs. Click the tab titled 'DELETE THIS PAGE'.
  4. Finally click the button titled 'I understand - please delete my page'.

When you click this button the page is immediately deleted.
