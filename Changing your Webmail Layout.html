Dreamhostâ€™s Webmail interface offers two themes: DreamHost Elastic (default) and DreamHost Larry.

The Larry theme lets you adjust the layout of the webmail client. The following steps allow you to enable and make changes to your webmail layout using the DreamHost Larry theme:

Changing your Layout

  1. Log into Webmail at https://webmail.dreamhost.com. Enter your username, password, and then click 'Login'.
  2. In the left pane, click 'Settings'.
  3. Choose 'Preferences'.
  4. Choose 'User Interface' in the left navigation bar.
  5. Under 'Interface skin', select 'DreamHost Larry' and click the Save button to save your changes.

Additional layout options

While using 'Larry', there are some additional layout options you can select, which are covered in the following steps:

  1. Open Webmail at https://webmail.dreamhost.com.
  2. Click the 'Settings' gear icon in the upper right of the main Webmail page
  3. Choose 'Preferences'.
  4. In the Settings menu, select the 'Mailbox View' section. Under 'Main Options', you can select the Widescreen, Desktop, or Link layouts.
  5. Click the DreamHost logo in the top left corner to return to the main Webmail page and view the new layout.

Changing your Skin back to the default

  1. Open Webmail at https://webmail.dreamhost.com.
  2. Click the 'Settings' gear icon in the upper right of the main Webmail page
  3. Choose 'Preferences'.
  4. Choose 'User Interface' in the left navigation bar.
  5. Under 'Interface skin', select 'DreamHost Elastic' and click the Save button to save your changes.
