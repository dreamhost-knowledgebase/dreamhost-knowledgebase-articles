Overview

OPcache is a caching engine built into PHP. When enabled, it dramatically increases the performance of websites that utilize PHP. OPcache is enabled by default on all DreamHost servers.

How to disable OPcache?

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the Manage button to open the Manage Websites page, which allows you to adjust various settings for your site.
    • If in Grid view, click the Manage button at the bottom of the website box.
    • If in List view, click the Manage button at the far right of your domain name.
  3. Scroll down to the Additional Settings section and click the Modify button on the Web Options row.
  4. Set the toggle to the right of PHP OpCache Support to unchecked.
  5. Click the Save Changes button to save your changes.
