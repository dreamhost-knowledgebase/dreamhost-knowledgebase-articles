Overview

This article explains how to delete a Discussion List within your DreamHost panel.

Deleting the list

  1. Navigate to the Discussion Lists page.
  2. Click the Delete button found under the Actions column to the right of the list.
    A confirmation box asks you to confirm the deletion, as well as any archived postings or email addresses associated with the list:
  3. Click the Delete Discussion List button to complete this process.

Do not delete a list until you are sure any necessary data is backed up. After the list is deleted, it may not be possible to restore the content.
