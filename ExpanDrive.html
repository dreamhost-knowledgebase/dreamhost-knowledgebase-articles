ExpanDrive is an app that allows you to connect to DreamObjects just like a USB drive. It is available for both Mac and Windows and allows you to access DreamObjects from any application on your computer. Before you begin, you’ll need to create a bucket on the (Panel > 'Cloud Services' > DreamObjects') page.

Mac version

  1. Download the latest release for Mac.
  2. Launch ExpanDrive and select the DreamObjects drive type.
  3. Enter the following:
    • Server: objects-us-east-1.dream.io

      This article uses the new DreamObjects cluster of 'objects-us-east-1.dream.io'. If you have an older DreamObjects account and have not migrated your data yet, your hostname may need to point to 'objects-us-west-1.dream.io instead. Please review the following migration article for further details.

      • What cluster is my DreamObjects data currently on?
    • Access Key ID: Your DreamObjects Access Key
    • Secret Access Key: Your DreamObjects Secret Key
    • Visit the DreamObjects keys article for details on finding your keys.
  4. Click the Connect button.
  5. You may see a window the first time you connect asking for you to enter you Mac user’s password. Enter it and click OK. This only needs to be done the first time you connect.
  6. The Finder window on your computer opens with your DreamObjects bucket. DreamObjects will now show as a mounted drive. You can see it visible in the top menu bar. You’ll also see it in open and save dialogs in your applications letting you easily access your DreamObjects data.

Windows version

  1. Download the latest release for Windows.
  2. Launch ExpanDrive and select the DreamObjects drive type.
  3. Enter the following:
    • Server: objects-us-east-1.dream.io

      This article uses the new DreamObjects cluster of 'objects-us-east-1.dream.io'. If you have an older DreamObjects account and have not migrated your data yet, your hostname may need to point to 'objects-us-west-1.dream.io instead. Please review the following migration article for further details.

      • What cluster is my DreamObjects data currently on?
    • Access Key ID: Your DreamObjects Access Key
    • Secret Access Key: Your DreamObjects Secret Key
    • Visit the DreamObjects keys article for details on finding your keys.
  4. Click the Connect button.
  5. ExpanDrive will attempt to connect to our ‘DreamObjects’ bucket.
  6. An explorer window on your computer opens with your DreamObjects buckets. Your DreamObjects data is now accessible as a mounted drive letting you easily access your DreamObjects data.
