Viewing your Remixer plan type

You can view all services billed to your account on the (Panel > 'Billing & Account' > 'Manage Account') page. Here you'll see which Remixer plan you're currently using.

How to cancel a Remixer plan

To cancel your Remixer plan, please email remixer@dreamhost.com. Make sure to write in from the primary email address linked to your DreamHost account. Support can then cancel the plan for you.
