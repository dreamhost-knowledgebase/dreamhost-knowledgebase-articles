Overview

The DreamHost online payment processor has a minimum amount it will accept for online payments. This minimum amount is currently $1.50 for Autopay, and $0.79 for manual payments.

What if my due balance is less than $1.50?

If your due balance for the month is lower than $1.50, DreamHost simply rolls your due balance over to the next month’s invoice. Until your balance is over $1.50 on an invoice, the amount will keep deferring the balance to the next month. Once your overall monthly due balance is $1.50 or higher, you can then pay off the full balance due.

What if I don’t want to have a small balance deferred each month?

If you have a balance of less than $1.50 and want to take care of that amount so you don't carry a balance, you can make a manual payment for what you owe, as long as it's at least $0.79 (which is the lowest amount that can be accepted). If you owe less than $0.79, you would still need to pay that amount and anything over the actual amount you owe will be applied to any future service fees.
