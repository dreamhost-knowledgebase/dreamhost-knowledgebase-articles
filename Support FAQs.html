I would like to attach a screenshot to my support ticket. How can I do that?

Attachments can only be added when submitting a ticket through the DreamHost panel. When creating a ticket, you have the ability to add the attachment. Regular email submissions with attachments to support are stripped of the attachment. Visit the following article for further details:

  • Attachments

What is the response time of technical support?

The support team strives to answer all emails within 24 hours. Most are answered in substantially less time.

What are the hours of DreamHost technical support?

  • DreamHost support is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  • Live Chat is also available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Phone support

Is there a phone number I can use to reach DreamHost or technical support?

DreamHost does not have a call-in phone number for technical support. Any contact must be made from the options in the following article:

  • Contacting DreamHost

Are call backs included in the account?

Support is mainly provided via email and LiveChat for free. Call back requests are available but not included in the hosting package. You must add them separately. Visit the following article for further details:

  • Phone support

General

What language does support offer?

Support is offered in English 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Support in Spanish is also offered via LiveChat 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Where is DreamHost support located?

Support is offered from 3 separate continents: North & South America, as well as Europe!
