Notes

  • Check out dreamhost.com for any current promo codes. Customers who add a site while a promotion is active get to keep the this special price even when the promotion is over. If the site is removed from DreamPress (Downgraded or Deleted), or if the customer's account is closed/disabled, they may lose the special promotional pricing.
  • Downgrading gives a credit for any unused portion of the billing period to be used on another hosting plan.
  • There are no refunds when canceling a DreamPress plan. View the 'Terms of Service' page for further details.

Additional domains

The original configuration of the Advanced plan comes with 3 sites only. This means you can have 1, 2, or 3 sites on the default 'Advanced' plan. If you want to add more than 3 sites, each new site is billed individually.

Non-Profit discount
