Overview

Some users need to add additional information to their invoices such as a 'Tax Identification Number' or 'VAT' number. The only way to add this information is to adjust the name of your account to include this number. It will then appear on your invoices.

The example below shows how to add a VAT number.

Adding the VAT number to your account name

By default, the Invoices in the panel do not display any VAT information. If you need to add this to your invoice, the only way would be to add the VAT number to your account name.

  1. Navigate to the Manage Account page.
  2. At the top, click the Rename button.
  3. In the 'New Account Name' field, enter your name followed by your VAT number.
  4. Click Change Name Now.
  5. Your VAT number will now appear on your invoice as your Account Name.
