Overview

There are two ways to reset your password:

  • In the DreamCompute panel
  • In the DreamHost panel

Changing your password in the DreamCompute panel

This option allows you to create your own password.

  1. Navigate to the DreamCompute page.
  2. Click the View Dashboard button.
  3. On the top right, click your username.
  4. From the dropdown select Settings.
  5. In the left menu under Settings, choose Change Password.
  6. When finished entering the new password, click the Change button on the bottom right.
  7. Please note that it may take a few minutes for the change to take affect.

Let the panel create a password for you

This option creates a random password for you.

  1. Navigate to the DreamCompute page.
  2. Click the Reset My Password link.
  3. A success message displays with your new password.
