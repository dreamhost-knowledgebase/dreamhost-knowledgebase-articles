Manage Domains

The Manage Domains page allows you to manage several aspects of your domain's hosting. Here you can add hosting, adjust the domain’s settings, and restore backed up web files.

View the following article for further details:

  • Manage Domains panel overview

Registrations

The Registrations page is where you can register a new domain and manage current domain registrations you have with DreamHost. Domain WHOIS modifications (including nameservers) can be made from here, as well as registration renewal settings and domain locking.

View the following article for further details:

  • Domain Registration

Reg. Transfer

The Reg. Transfer page is where you can transfer a domain registration to or from DreamHost, as well as obtaining the domain’s authorization code.

View the following articles for further details:

  • Transfer a domain registration away from DreamHost
  • Transfer a domain registration to DreamHost
