Overview

If you have a QNAP network attached storage (NAS) device, you can back it up to DreamObjects for disaster recovery and long-term archiving.

Installation

Log in to your QNAP device and launch the App Center. Search for DreamObjects in the search box and click the app. Click the button to add it to your device.

Configuration

To use the DreamObjects app, you’ll need to either sign in with your existing DreamHost account or create a new one. If you’re new to DreamHost, click the link to create an account. Otherwise, follow the link to sign in to the DreamHost control panel and authorize your QNAP device on your account.
