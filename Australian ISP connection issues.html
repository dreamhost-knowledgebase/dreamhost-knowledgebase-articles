Some ISPs in Australia require their customers use their outbound mail servers and block access to any DreamHost SMTP servers. Telstra Bigpond is a popular example.

Telstra Bigpond

Telstra Bigpond blocks the use of any outbound mail servers other than their own. Many hotels and motels in Australia use Telstra Bigpond for their in-house WiFi.

View the following link for details on POP/IMAP/SMTP settings:

  • https://support.telstra.com.au/t5/Set-Up/IMAP-POP-and-SMTP-information-for-manual-email-setup/ta-p/398464
