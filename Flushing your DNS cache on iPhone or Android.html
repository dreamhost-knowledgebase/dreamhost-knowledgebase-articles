iPhone

Option 1 — Toggle Airplane mode

The easiest solution is to toggle Airplane mode on and off.

  1. Open 'Settings'.
  2. You will see the icon of an airplane. Click this to toggle on.
  3. Click it again to toggle off.

Option 2 — Reboot your device

Rebooting should also clear out your DNS.

Option 3 — Dump Network settings

This will clear your cache, but will also momentarily disable your Wi-Fi connection.

  1. Open 'Settings'.
  2. Click 'General'.
  3. Click 'Reset', then 'Reset Network Settings'.

Your device is then rebooted. If you had previously customized your DNS servers, you must manually reset them again.

Android (8.0 Oreo)

Clear cache in a browser

Use this option to clear cache in a browser. This shows how to clear cache in Chrome. This will NOT clear your login data.

  1. Swipe down from the top.
  2. On the top right, click the Gear icon.
  3. In the first section of options, click 'Apps'.
  4. Scroll down and click Chrome.
  5. On the Chrome settings page, click 'Storage'.
  6. Click the 'Clear Cache' button.
