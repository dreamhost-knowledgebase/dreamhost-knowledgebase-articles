DreamPress offers several types of payment plans depending on your needs.

Payment cycle — Select either an annual pre-paid plan, or a monthly plan.

Server storage — Select between 10GB, 30GB, 50GB, or 80GB of storage space.

Plan type — Choose between a 'Basic', 'Plus', or 'Advanced' plan. (view plans below)

Basic plan

Payment cycle

If you choose an annual pre-paid plan, the monthly cost breaks down to $16.95/month.

If you choose to pay month to month, the monthly cost is $19.95/month.

Number of sites allowed

Only one site is allowed on a 'Basic' plan.

Storage space

10GB of storage is available.

Jetpack

The free version of Jetpack is available.

Plus plan

Payment cycle

If you choose an annual pre-paid plan, the monthly cost breaks down to $24.95/month.

If you choose to pay month to month, the monthly cost is $29.95/month.

Number of sites allowed

Only one site is allowed on a 'Basic' plan.

Storage space

30GB of storage is available. You can also upgrade to 50GB with an additional $3.95/month for annual plans, or $4.95/month for monthly plans.

Jetpack

The premium version of Jetpack is available.

Extras

The 'Plus' plan also includes the following:

  • Daily offisite backups with unlimited storage.
  • Daily malware scanning.
  • Akismet spam protection.
  • 3 technical support call-backs per month.

Advanced plan

Payment cycle

If you choose an annual pre-paid plan, the monthly cost breaks down to $35.95/month for.

If you choose to pay month to month, the monthly cost is $35.95/month.

Number of sites allowed

3 or more.

Storage space

30GB of storage is available. You can also upgrade to 50GB with an additional $3.95/month for annual plans, or $4.95/month for monthly plans. An 80GB plan is also available for an additional $8.95/month for annual plans, or $9.95/month for monthly plans.

Jetpack

The premium version of Jetpack is available.

Extras

The 'Plus' plan also includes the following:

  • Daily offisite backups with unlimited storage.
  • Daily malware scanning.
  • Akismet spam protection.
  • 5 technical support call-backs per month.

Notes

  • Check out dreamhost.com for any current promo codes. Customers who add a site while a promotion is active get to keep the this special price even when the promotion is over. If the site is removed from DreamPress (Downgraded or Deleted), or if the customer's account is closed/disabled, they may lose the special promotional pricing.
  • Downgrading gives a credit for any unused portion of the billing period to be used on another hosting plan.
  • There are no refunds when canceling a DreamPress plan. View the 'Terms of Service' page for further details.
