Overview

You can upload audio to your page as long as you've first added a 'Web Section' that supports audio. View the following article for instructions on how to add a section that supports audio:

  • Adding a new 'Web Section' to a page

Upload audio into Remixer

Remixer allows you to upload video from SoundCloud. Once uploaded, you can add them to your site. To upload:

  1. Click the 'Media' button on the top right.
  2. Click the Audio link to view your options to upload.
  3. Click on any option and upload your images.

Editing audio

  1. Edit your Remixer page.
  2. Add a 'Web Section' to the page that supports audio.
  3. Click the sample audio to edit.
    An audio toolbar appears below, where you can import audio from SoundCloud (may be subject to copyright).
