Overview

The following describes the eligibility requirements to sign up for G Suite at DreamHost.

DreamHost G Suite Eligibility

In order to sign up for G Suite through your DreamHost account:

  • Your domain must be registered or hosted (either Fully Hosted, Parked, Redirect, or 'DNS Only') within your DreamHost account.
  • You must live in a supported country (see Google's supported country list here).
  • The domain cannot be actively using G Suite (or its predecessor, Google Apps).
  • The domain cannot have been used with a G Suite trial within the last 90 days.
