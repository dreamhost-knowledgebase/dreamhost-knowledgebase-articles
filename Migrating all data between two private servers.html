Overview

If you have two VPS machines, you can use the panel to migrate all data from one to the other. This assumes you've just added a brand new private server.

Migrating

  1. Navigate to the VPS page.
  2. To the right of the server you wish to delete, click the Delete button.
  3. You are presented with a page that offers you the option to migrate all data to a different server. In the screenshot above, everything is being migrated to a shared server.
  4. Click Move everything on ps### to <newserver> and then destroy ps###!. This ensures your data is copied to the new server where you'll continue to host your site.

Your data will begin to migrate to the new private server. This could take some time depending on how much data there is to transfer.
