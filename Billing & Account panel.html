Overview

The Billing & Account section of your panel allows you to maintain and monitor almost every aspect of your account. In this section you can make payments, view invoices, set up security measures, backup your account, create rewards, and view usage.

Manage Account

The Manage Account page provides you with a complete overview of your panel, including:

  • Account # and name
  • Current amount due
  • Current Promotions
  • Service Plans
  • Domains Registered

Under the section Other Active Services, click the View Services button to view the following.

  • Servers
  • Users
  • Mailboxes
  • Domains Hosted
  • Databases

You can close your account from this page.

Account Privileges

On the Account Privileges page, you can add specific email addresses and decide exactly what they can access in your panel. This gives you the ability to provide selected access to your panel.

View the following article for details:

  • Account Privileges

Privacy Settings

The Privacy Settings page opens your privacy portal, where you can adjust which emails subscriptions you wish to receive, click to view privacy statements, and view a list of third parties where data is shared.

Manage Payments

The Manage Payments page allows you to make a payment.

You can also add or change the details of your payment method by using the following:

  • Setup AutoPay (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Billing Details (includes amount due)
  • Other payment options

View the following article for further details:

  • Payment methods

View Invoices

On the View Invoices page, you can print or view invoices. You can also view the complete record of charges and credits posted to your account.

View the following article for details:

  • Invoices overview

Security

On the Security page, there are 5 Sections:

  • Change Password – Where you can change your panel.dreamhost.com password
  • Multifactor Authentication – Provides additional security to your panel login
  • Change Secret Question & Answer – These Q&As are used to retrieve your password
  • IP locking – Use to allow only certain IPs to view your site
  • Active sessions – Lists details of what IP addresses accessed your panel and at what time.

View the following links for details:

  • Security related articles
  • Enabling Multifactor Authentication
  • Security panel page overview

Backup Your Account

The Backup Your Account page provides an easy way to generate a complete account backup.

Bandwidth Usage

The Bandwidth Usage page lists stats on bandwidth usage by domain.

Disk Usage

The Disk Usage page lists disk usage by user, fully hosted domain, email address, database, and Discussion List.
