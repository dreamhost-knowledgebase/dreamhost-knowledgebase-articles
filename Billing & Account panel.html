Overview

The Billing & Account section of your panel allows you to maintain and monitor almost every aspect of your account. In this section, you can view plan details, make payments,  and view invoices.

Manage Account

The Manage Account page provides you with a complete overview of your panel, including:

  • Account # and name
  • Current amount due
  • Active Plans
  • Domain Registrations

Under the section Other Active Services, click the View Services button to view the following.

  • Hosted Domains
  • Mailboxes
  • Users
  • Servers
  • Databases

You can close your account from this page.

Manage Payments

The Manage Payments page allows you to make a payment.

You can also add or change the details of your payment method by using the following:

  • Setup AutoPay (credit/debit card or PayPal)
  • Billing Details (includes amount due)
  • Other payment options

View the following article for further details:

  • Payment methods

View Invoices

On the View Invoices page, you can print or view invoices. You can also view the complete record of charges and credits posted to your account.
