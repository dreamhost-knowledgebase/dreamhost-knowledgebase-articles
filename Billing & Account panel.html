The Billing & Account section of your panel allows you to maintain and monitor almost every aspect of your account. In this section you can make payments, view invoices, set up security measures, backup your account, create rewards, and view usage.

Manage Account

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Manage Account’) page provides you with a complete overview of your panel, including:

  • Account # and name
  • Current amount due
  • Current Promotions
  • Service Plans
  • Servers
  • Users
  • Mailboxes
  • Domains Hosted
  • Domains Registered
  • Databases
  • Unique IP Addresses

Make Payment

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Make Payment’ ) page allows you to make a payment.

You can also add or change the details of your payment method by using the following:

  • Account balance at the top
  • AutoPay in the center
  • Single payments at the bottom

View the following article for further details:

  • Payment methods

View Invoices

On the (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘View Invoices’) page, you can print or view invoices. You can also view the complete record of charges and credits posted to your account.

View the following article for details:

  • Invoices overview

Security

On the (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Security’) page, there are 5 Sections:

  • Change Password – Where you can change your panel.dreamhost.com password
  • Multifactor Authentication – Provides additional security to your panel login
  • Change Secret Question & Answer – These Q&As are used to retrieve your password
  • IP locking – Use to allow only certain IPs to view your site
  • Active sessions – Lists details of what IP addresses accessed your panel and at what time.

View the following links for details:

  • Security related articles
  • Enabling Multifactor Authentication

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Backup Your Account’) page provides an easy way to generate a complete account backup.

Affiliates

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Affiliates’) page provides information on the program, which is a way to receive credit or cash for referring customers to DreamHost.

View the following page for details:

  • Affiliates / Rewards / Promos

Buy Gift Card

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Buy Gift Card’) page allows you to purchase a gift card used for DreamHost services.

Bandwidth Usage

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Bandwidth Usage’) page lists stats on bandwidth usage by domain.

Disk Usage

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘Disk Usage’) page lists disk usage by user, fully hosted domain, email address, database, and discussion list.

MySQL Usage

The (Panel > ‘Billing & Account’ > ‘MySQL Usage’) page provides information on MySQL usage by database, disk usage, connects, queries, and conueries.
