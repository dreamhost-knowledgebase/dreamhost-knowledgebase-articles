Overview

This article describes how to adjust the type of DreamPress plan you're currently running.

If, instead, you wish to cancel DreamPress and move your site to another hosting plan, visit the following article for more information:

  • Downgrading DreamPress to another hosting plan

You must have an active hosting plan in order to downgrade and move your DreamPress install.

Changing the plan

To make changes to your DreamPress Plan:

  1. Navigate to the Manage Websites page.
  2. Click the Manage button to open the Domain Settings page, which allows you to adjust various settings for your site.
    • If in Grid view, click the Manage button at the bottom of the website box.
    • If in List view, click the Manage button at the far right of your domain name.
  3. Click the Hosting tab.
    Your DreamPress plan page displays.
  4. Select from the following options:
    • Select an annual or monthly term
    • Choose a DreamPress plan type.
  5. Review the details for your new plan, then click the Change Plan button to save the changes.
