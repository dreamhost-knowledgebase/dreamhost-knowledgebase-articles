Overview

Google is changing the name of its G Suite product to Google Workspace. See the following article for more information:

  • G Suite is now Google Workspace

Canceling Google Workspace is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you wish to proceed before canceling your Google Workspace Subscription with DreamHost.

You may cancel your Google Workspace plan at any time after your 30-day free trial has expired.

Canceling your Google Workspace subscription with DreamHost ends your relationship with your Google Workspace account as purchased and managed through DreamHost. Google will then begin billing you directly if you'd like to continue service with them.

Do NOT cancel your Google Workspace account to fix a problem

Do not cancel your Google Workspace account to resolve any configuration issues you're having with it. If you cancel and create a new plan, your configuration issues will remain as they currently are.

Contact DreamHost support to resolve any problems you're having with your account. This is the only way to ensure your account is configured correctly.

What happens to your account when you cancel

The following occurs when you cancel your Google Workspace account in the DreamHost panel.

  • Billing through DreamHost stops.
  • Your Google Workspace account is transferred to Google.
  • Google begins to bill you directly for Google Workspace.
  • Your MX (mail) DNS records will stay pointed to Google in case you wish to set up services directly with them. If you wish to switch to DreamHost-hosted email, go to the Custom MX page in your DreamHost panel and click Make Me Regular.

Steps to take before canceling

Before you cancel, make sure you've done the following:

  • Save user data
  • Save billing records
  • Delete Google Workspace Marketplace apps
  • Check if you upgraded from business email powered by Google Workspace

The following link goes into more details about the steps above:

  • https://support.google.com/a/answer/1257646

Transfer data between Google Workspace accounts

As an administrator, you can transfer your users' data from the current account to the new one. View the following article for further information.

  • https://support.google.com/a/answer/1041297?hl=en

Canceling in your panel to stop billing

When you cancel your subscription in the DreamHost panel, billing is immediately stopped for Google Workspace services.

You must cancel your Google Workspace subscription in the DreamHost panel to stop all future payments. If you cancel within Google Workspace console only, you will continue to be billed for the services.

  • https://support.google.com/a/answer/1257646

Canceling your Business plan

If you cancel your Google Workspace Business plan, you must wait 60 days before you are able to sign up for any new edition of Google Workspace.

Canceling if you have other types of subscriptions

If you have multiple subscriptions (such as Cloud Identity or Chrome Enterprise) and cancel only your Google Workspace subscription, your other subscriptions will remain active.

Canceling your Google Workspace subscription

  1. Navigate to the Google Workspace page in your panel.
  2. Click the Manage Plan button to the right of your domain.
  3. Click the Cancel Plan button.
  4. Type in the word DELETE, then click Confirm to cancel.
  5. A success message displays confirming your plan has been canceled.

MX records change

When Google Workspace is deleted, your MX (mail) records immediately change back to DreamHost. To access your email address, you must either use Webmail, or an email client.

  • Email client configuration overview

Email notification

Once canceled, you will receive an email with the following content.

Canceling your Google Workspace subscription with DreamHost ends our reseller relationship with your Google Workspace account. Google will begin billing you directly if you'd like to continue service with them - there is a short grace period in effect to ensure you don't experience any interruption in service.

At this point, you may choose to:

  • Set up billing with Google to ensure uninterrupted service
  • Upgrade to another Google Workspace plan
  • Completely cancel your account

To take the next steps, log into your Google Workspace Admin Console:

  • https://admin.google.com/

For additional assistance, please consult Google's official help documentation:

  • https://support.google.com/a/#topic=7570177

Steps after you have canceled

Support assistance

After your Google Workspace plan has been canceled in the DreamHost panel, DreamHost technical support will no longer be able to assist with your account. You must contact Google and/or their documentation for any further assistance.

Setting up billing

You may continue to log in normally at https://admin.google.com to manage your account through Google. After logging in, make sure that you have set up billing directly with Google in order for your services to not be interrupted.
