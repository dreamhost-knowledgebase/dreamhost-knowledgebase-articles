Overview

  • G Suite is now Google Workspace

DreamHost supports monthly billing. Monthly pricing breaks down as follows:

Plan type Storage Cost per month
Business Starter 30 GB $6 per user
Business Standard 2 TB $12 per user

Free trial

When adding Google Workspace to a domain, you may be eligible for a 30 day free trial for up to 10 licenses. If you are eligible, you will start with a 30 day trial.

If you are not eligible for a trial, you should contact DreamHost support. Support can then determine if you are a direct customer of Google or another reseller, then manually enable the service on a domain via the reseller console if needed.

After the trial period is over

After the free trial period, you are not immediately billed for Google Workspace. The charge is added to your regular monthly bill and due at the next due date (rebill date).

At any point during the trial, you may cancel the service. Upon the completion of the trial, you will automatically transition onto monthly billing.

Users

You must purchase users in the DreamHost panel. You can then add them in the Google Workspace console.

For example, you are billed based on the number of users purchased in the DreamHost control panel. You will not be able to add more users in the G Suite admin console than the number purchased in our control panel.

If you need more users, you must first purchase users in the DreamHost control panel. You will then be able to add users up to the purchased limit in the Google Workspace admin console.

Additional users purchased mid-month are pro-rated on a daily basis.

Removing users

You are able to remove users in the Google Workspace admin console at any time. If you do not want to be billed for unused users, you will need to decrease the number of users in the DreamHost control panel.

Users removed mid-month will be credited on a pro-rated daily basis.

Billing is prorated when adjusting users

Google Workspace plans are based on number of users. For example, switching from a 2 user plan to a 10 user plan will result in the previous plan ending and being prorated, and the new plan starting immediately.

Non-Payment and suspension (7 days)

DreamHost will send you a message if your account is past due. You will have 7 days to then pay the past due payment.

If they do not pay the outstanding balance by the end of the 7th day, DreamHost will suspend your Google Workspace account.

Once you have paid the balance in full, Google Workspace will be re-enabled.

If your account is suspended for 60 days, it is automatically canceled.
